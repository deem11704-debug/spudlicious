🥔 Chapter 1 · The Beginning
🥔 Our Story

More Than a Potato —
It's an Experience.

Born in Busaiteen, built for Bahrain. Spudlicious is on a mission to prove the humble potato can be extraordinary.

We started Spudlicious with one bold idea: the potato is the most underrated food in the world. Crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside — then we load it with the richest, creamiest toppings you've ever tasted.

Every spud we serve is baked to perfection, split open, and stuffed with signature sauces made from scratch. No shortcuts. No compromise. Just pure, loaded goodness — 100% Halal, every time.

From late-night cravings to corporate catering — Spudlicious is where every potato becomes a masterpiece. And we're just getting started.

The person behind the spud

Meet the man who
reinvented the potato.

An engineer with an entrepreneur's mind, Abdulrahman Alhamad took a comfort food everyone knows — and turned it into an experience Bahrain won't forget.

“Every great business begins with a simple idea. Mine started with a passion for creating something that brings people together.”

I wanted to take a comfort food that everyone knows and reinvent it — bold flavors, quality ingredients, a real identity of its own. That idea became Spudlicious.

By profession I'm an engineer, but I've always had an entrepreneurial mind. Engineering taught me to think strategically, solve problems, and obsess over every detail. Entrepreneurship gave me the chance to turn that creativity into something real. I believe success comes from pairing discipline with innovation.

Spudlicious is more than a loaded-potato concept — it's a reflection of my commitment to quality, consistency, and the people we serve. From the recipes to the brand, every decision is made with one goal: an experience that keeps people coming back. From the first bite to the last, I want every customer to leave with a smile and a reason to return.

Great brands are never finished. They evolve — through continuous improvement, through listening, and through the will to keep raising the standard.

❓ FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about Spudlicious.

🤔 Ask away!
Is Spudlicious 100% Halal?
+

Yes, absolutely. Every single ingredient — from our meats to our sauces — is 100% Halal certified. No exceptions, ever. We take this very seriously.

How do I place an order?
+

Simply add items to your cart on this page and tap "Send Order via WhatsApp" — we'll confirm and get your spud ready. Or swing by the truck directly in Busaiteen!

Where exactly are you located?
+

We're based in Busaiteen, Kingdom of Bahrain. Check our map section below for directions. You can also DM us on Instagram @spudlicious.bh for our exact spot each day.

What are your opening hours?
+

We typically operate evenings from 7PM onwards. Follow @spudlicious.bh on Instagram for daily updates on hours and location.

Do you cater for events?
+

Absolutely! We love catering for corporate events, weddings, private parties, schools, universities, and festivals. Use the booking form above or WhatsApp us to discuss your event.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept Cash, BenefitPay, and KNET. Easy and convenient — no complicated checkout, just great food.
